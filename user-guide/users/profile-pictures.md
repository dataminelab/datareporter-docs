---
title: Adding a Profile Picture
section: user-guide
category: users
---

If you login to DataReporter with a Google account, DataReporter will automatically fetch your Google Account profile picture. Alternatively, DataReporter uses [Gravatar](https://en.gravatar.com/) to handle profile pictures.

If you don't have a Gravatar account, sign up for Gravatar with the email address you used to sign-in to DataReporter.

Gravatars are cached by your browser. Once signed up or updated, your Gravatar will appear on DataReporter within 5 minutes after the change following a page refresh.
